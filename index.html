Projects

  • Mental health Analysis using Power BI

    This project analyzes a structured mental health survey dataset containing 1,998 cleaned responses with 21 demographic, lifestyle, behavioral, and psychological features. Each record is labeled with one of twelve clinically motivated depression types, enabling analysis for multi-class depression classification.

    Using Microsoft Power BI, interactive dashboards were created to explore relationships between demographic factors, lifestyle behaviors, and psychological indicators with different depression categories. The visualizations highlight the distribution of depression types, behavioral patterns, and key influencing factors, while interactive filters allow users to dynamically explore the data.

    The project demonstrates how data visualization and BI tools can support mental health analytics and exploratory insights for depression classification.

  • Amazon sales data analysis using Power BI

    This project outlines a comprehensive **Amazon Sales Performance & Customer Insights Dashboard** built in Power BI. It is divided into two main analytical areas:
    1. Sales & Profitability Analysis – Focused on evaluating overall business performance through key KPIs such as total revenue, profit, profit margin, and total orders. It explores sales trends over time, category-level performance, the impact of discounts on profitability, and payment method distribution to uncover revenue-driving factors and margin risks.
    2. Customer & Product Performance Analysis – Concentrates on understanding customer behavior and product success by analyzing ratings, reviews, revenue contribution, and regional performance. It examines relationships between ratings, reviews, revenue, and profit to determine whether higher customer satisfaction leads to stronger financial results.
    Overall, the dashboard aims to deliver actionable insights that support data-driven decisions in pricing, discount strategies, product optimization, and customer engagement.
